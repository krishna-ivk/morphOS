# Job Search Starter Kit

This folder is the working area for automating your job search with JobOps and tightening your LinkedIn profile around the same source data.

## What goes where

- `profile-source-of-truth.md`
  - Fill this in first.
  - This becomes the master input for your resume, LinkedIn, and JobOps scoring.
- `linkedin-optimization.md`
  - Use this to rewrite your public LinkedIn profile.
  - It mirrors the same fields so your profile stays consistent with your resume.
- `jobops-setup.md`
  - Follow this to run JobOps locally and connect the pieces needed for automation.

## Recommended order

1. Complete `profile-source-of-truth.md`
2. Update LinkedIn using `linkedin-optimization.md`
3. Build or refresh your base resume in RxResume
4. Launch JobOps and connect:
   - LLM provider
   - RxResume
   - Gmail
5. Start with small LinkedIn-focused searches, then tune filters and scoring

## Why this structure works

JobOps scores jobs against your resume/profile. If your LinkedIn, resume, and project descriptions all tell the same story, you get:

- better relevance scoring
- cleaner tailored resumes
- easier networking outreach
- less manual rewriting per application
