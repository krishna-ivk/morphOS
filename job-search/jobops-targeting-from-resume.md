## Scoring instructions to add in JobOps

Prefer roles that involve:

- owning or improving data platforms
- Spark, Databricks, or large-scale pipeline work
- data quality, governance, and reliability
- applied AI or LLM workflows tied to business decisions
- measurable platform or cost impact

Deprioritize roles that are:

- mostly dashboard-only analytics
- pure reporting without platform ownership
- generic ETL support roles with little systems design
- mostly frontend or application development
