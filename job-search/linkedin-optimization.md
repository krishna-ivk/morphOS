# LinkedIn Optimization Worksheet

Use this after `profile-source-of-truth.md` is complete.

The goal is not to sound generic. The goal is to make your profile easy for recruiters and hiring managers to understand in under 20 seconds.

## Headline formula

Use:

`Target role | strongest domain / stack | business outcome`

Examples:

- `Backend Engineer | TypeScript, Node.js, Postgres | Building reliable APIs and automation systems`
- `Full-Stack Engineer | React, Python, AI workflows | Shipping internal tools that cut manual ops`
- `ML Engineer | LLM apps, evaluation, Python | Turning AI prototypes into production systems`

## About section formula

Write 5 short paragraphs:

1. Who you are and what you do
2. Your strongest technical lane
3. The kind of outcomes you create
4. Proof via 2 to 3 concrete wins
5. The roles you want next

Template:

```text
I’m a [target role] with [X years] of experience building [systems/products/domain].

My strongest work sits at the intersection of [strength 1], [strength 2], and [strength 3]. I’m especially effective when a team needs [type of problem solved].

In recent work, I’ve helped teams [outcome 1], [outcome 2], and [outcome 3].

Highlights include [achievement with metric], [achievement with metric], and [achievement with metric].

I’m currently focused on [target roles / domain / location preference].
```

## Experience bullets

For each role, prefer this pattern:

- action + scope + tech + result

Examples:

- `Built internal automation workflows in TypeScript and Python that reduced manual triage time by 60%.`
- `Led backend delivery for a multi-tenant platform serving 20k+ monthly users across payments and analytics flows.`
- `Designed CI and test automation that cut deployment failures and improved release confidence for the engineering team.`

## Featured section

Add 3 to 5 links:

- best project demo
- GitHub repo
- portfolio or case study
- standout post
- resume PDF

## Skills section

Pin the skills that support your target role, not every tool you have touched.

Target mix:

- 3 core role skills
- 3 stack skills
- 3 domain or product skills

Example for backend:

- Backend Development
- API Design
- Distributed Systems
- TypeScript
- Node.js
- PostgreSQL
- AWS
- Docker
- System Design

## Profile photo and banner

- Photo: clear, well-lit, plain background, shoulders-up
- Banner: one line of positioning plus 2 to 4 keywords

Suggested banner copy formula:

`[Target role] | [stack/domain] | [value created]`

## Creator / networking layer

Post lightly but consistently. You do not need to become a full-time content creator.

Good starter topics:

- a project teardown
- what you learned shipping something difficult
- a before/after engineering improvement
- notes from a system design problem you solved
- career transition story with proof

## Final check before updating LinkedIn

- Headline matches your target job titles
- About section includes metrics
- Top 3 experience entries focus on impact
- Featured links are live
- Skills support your next role
- Open to Work settings match your real preferences
